How to Use GestureConnect

Sign to Text

Click on 'Sign to Text' to open the camera. Perform sign language gestures, and the system will convert them into text in real-time.

Sign to Speech

Click 'Start' to activate the sign recognition system. Once a gesture is detected, the system will convert it into audible speech.

Speech to Text

Press the 'Start Listening' button and speak into the microphone. The spoken words will be transcribed into text in a text box.

Text to Speech

Enter text into the provided box and press 'Play'. The system will read out the text in a natural-sounding voice.
